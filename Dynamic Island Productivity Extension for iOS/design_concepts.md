# Focus Flow: 10 Design Concepts

## 1. Ethereal Minimalism (Current)
*   **Vibe:** Calm, flow-state, weightless.
*   **Visuals:** Soft gradients (Purple/Lavender/Mint), blurs, no hard borders.
*   **Animation:** Fluid, slow-moving, organic ripples.
*   **Font:** General Sans (Clean, modern).

## 2. Neo-Brutalism
*   **Vibe:** Bold, loud, unapologetic.
*   **Visuals:** High contrast, thick black borders (2px-3px), hard shadows (no blur), vibrant neon accents (Lime/Hot Pink).
*   **Animation:** Snappy, instant, bouncy (high stiffness).
*   **Font:** Space Grotesk or similar bold grotesque.

## 3. Cyberpunk / Glitch
*   **Vibe:** High-tech, dystopian, data-driven.
*   **Visuals:** Dark background, neon cyan/magenta text, scanlines, digital noise.
*   **Animation:** Glitch effects on state changes, text decoding (hacker style).
*   **Font:** JetBrains Mono (Monospace).

## 4. Soft Clay (Claymorphism)
*   **Vibe:** Friendly, tactile, playful.
*   **Visuals:** 3D look with inner shadows and soft outer shadows, rounded, pastel colors (Bubblegum Pink, Sky Blue).
*   **Animation:** Squishy, elastic, rubber-band feel.
*   **Font:** Rounded Sans (e.g., Varela Round or similar).

## 5. Retro Pixel
*   **Vibe:** Nostalgic, arcade, 8-bit.
*   **Visuals:** Pixelated icons, blocky progress bars, limited color palette (GameBoy greens or CGA).
*   **Animation:** Frame-by-frame stepping, no smooth transitions.
*   **Font:** Press Start 2P or pixel font.

## 6. Frosted Glass (Glassmorphism)
*   **Vibe:** Premium, icy, modern iOS.
*   **Visuals:** High background blur (backdrop-filter), semi-transparent white borders, subtle gradients.
*   **Animation:** Smooth sliding, glass refraction effects.
*   **Font:** SF Pro (System default).

## 7. Organic Nature
*   **Vibe:** Grounded, peaceful, eco-friendly.
*   **Visuals:** Earth tones (Sage Green, Terracotta, Sand), leaf-shaped masks or accents, paper texture.
*   **Animation:** Growing/blooming effects, wind-like swaying.
*   **Font:** Serif or Humanist Sans.

## 8. Industrial Tech
*   **Vibe:** Precision, engineering, blueprint.
*   **Visuals:** Grid lines, technical markings, safety orange/yellow accents, dark grey matte finish.
*   **Animation:** Mechanical sliding, sliding doors, robotic ease-in-out.
*   **Font:** DIN or Technical Monospace.

## 9. Pop Art
*   **Vibe:** Energetic, comic book, expressive.
*   **Visuals:** Halftone patterns, bold black outlines, primary colors (Red, Yellow, Blue), "Pow!" style shapes.
*   **Animation:** Jerky, comic-strip panel transitions.
*   **Font:** Comic-style bold font.

## 10. Zen Ink
*   **Vibe:** Minimalist, traditional, artistic.
*   **Visuals:** Black and white only, brush stroke textures, rough edges, rice paper background.
*   **Animation:** Ink spreading, fading in/out like watercolor.
*   **Font:** Calligraphic or clean Serif.
